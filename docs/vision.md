# Material Passport Vision

## Problem

Material information is fragmented across spreadsheets, PDFs, BIM models, and proprietary systems.

Organizations struggle with:

* Material traceability
* Sustainability reporting
* Circular economy tracking
* Reuse and recycling analysis

## Users

* Architects
* Construction Companies
* Sustainability Consultants
* BIM Engineers
* Material Manufacturers
* Building Owners

## Why This Project Exists

Material Passport aims to provide an open-source platform for creating and managing digital material passports that improve transparency, sustainability, and circularity across the building lifecycle.
